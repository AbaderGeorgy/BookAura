To borrow a book, simply navigate to the 'Books' section, find a book you're interested in, and click on it to view details. On the book details page, you'll find options to borrow or read online.

The standard loan period for all our e-books is 14 days. You will receive a reminder email two days before your loan expires.

With a standard membership, you can borrow up to 5 books at a time. Premium members can borrow up to 10 books simultaneously.

Books are automatically returned at the end of the loan period, so you don't have to worry about late fees! Your access to the e-book will simply expire.
